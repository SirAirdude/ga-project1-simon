Instructions

  1. Press the Play button to begin the game.
  2. The buttons around the start button will flash in sequence
  3. Press the buttons is the same order
  4. If you are correct, the buttons will flash the sequence again, adding one more flash to the end of the sequence
  5. The game will keep going util you get the sequence wrong, where it will give you your score.
